### PH Engagement Strategy

- **First 4 hours are critical** — be active, respond to every comment
- **Authentic engagement** — answer technical questions with depth, acknowledge limitations honestly
- **Thank upvoters** — don't ask for upvotes, but do engage with supporters
- **Don't game it** — no "please upvote" messages to your network; share the link and let people decide
- **Address concerns directly** — if someone raises a privacy or technical concern, respond with specific docs/code references

## 4. Hacker News Show HN

**Title:**

```
Show HN: Finance – Offline-first budget tracker with on-device encryption (KMP, 4 platforms)
```

**Post:**

> I've been building Finance, a personal budget tracker where the core architectural decision is: your financial data stays on your device.
>
> Tech stack:
>
> - Shared logic: Kotlin Multiplatform (KMP)
> - iOS: SwiftUI | Android: Jetpack Compose | Web: React PWA | Windows: Compose Desktop
> - Local DB: SQLDelight + SQLCipher (AES-256-GCM)
> - Sync: PowerSync (opt-in, E2E for sensitive fields)
> - Auth: Passkeys (WebAuthn/FIDO2)
> - Backend: Supabase (PostgreSQL + Edge Functions + RLS)
>
> Key decisions:
>
> - No Plaid/bank connections — manual entry first (30-second daily habit)
> - Hybrid E2E encryption — sensitive fields (amounts, balances, notes) encrypted end-to-end; sync metadata (timestamps, categories) server-readable
> - Expertise-tiered UI — three levels change terminology, feature visibility, and chart complexity
> - Cognitive accessibility as a design principle — reduced motion, simplified views, non-judgmental language
>
> The core tracker is free forever — accounts, transactions, envelope budgets, goals, reports. Premium adds AI insights and multi-device sync.
>
> Source: [GitHub link] (BSL 1.1)
> Architecture docs: [link to ADR-0004]
> Download: [links]
>
> Happy to discuss architecture decisions, the KMP experience in production, or the privacy model. What would you poke holes in?

### Template Responses

**Positive feedback:**

> Thank you! Really glad [specific thing they mentioned] is working for you. If you run into any issues, we're here. 🙂

**Technical question:**

> Great question! Here's how it works: [specific answer]. Full details in our architecture docs: [link].

**"How do you make money?"**

> Optional premium subscriptions (~$4.99/mo). Premium adds AI insights, multi-device sync, and household sharing. The core tracker is free forever — no ads, no data selling, no trial periods.

## 8. Contingency Plans

### If App Store Review Is Delayed

- **Impact:** iOS listing not live on launch day
- **Response:** Launch on all other platforms, update social copy to say "iOS coming in [timeframe]," resubmit if rejected and fix issues
- **Communication:** "We're launching on Android, Web, and Windows today. iOS is in Apple's review process and will be live shortly."

### If a Critical Bug Is Found on Launch Day

- **Impact:** Core feature broken, bad first impression
- **Response:** Engineering hotfix priority. Marketing pauses "download now" messaging. Post transparent update on social.
- **Communication:** "We found a bug in [feature] and are fixing it now. Update coming within [timeframe]. Your data is safe."

### If Negative Press or Viral Criticism Occurs

- **Impact:** Negative narrative before positive one is established
- **Response:** Respond factually and quickly. Don't get defensive. Acknowledge valid points. Correct inaccuracies with specific references.
- **Communication:** Direct, specific, documented. Link to code and docs for technical claims.

### If Product Hunt Underperforms

- **Impact:** Lower initial visibility than hoped
- **Response:** Focus on organic channels (HN, Reddit, blog). PH is one channel, not the only channel. Content and community drive long-term growth.
- **Communication:** No change to external messaging. Internally: adjust expectations, increase organic posting cadence.

### Marketing Pause Trigger

If engineering needs to halt the rollout:

- [ ] Marketing team is notified immediately via [channel]
- [ ] All "download now" CTAs are paused or modified
- [ ] Social posts are paused (don't delete, just stop posting new ones)
- [ ] PH and HN posts are updated with transparent status
- [ ] Existing posts are NOT deleted (transparency > damage control)
